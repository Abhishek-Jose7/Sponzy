Getting Started

Follow these steps to create your account:

  1. Click the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner
  2. Choose your account type (Seeker or Sponsor)
  3. Fill in your details
  4. Verify your email address
  5. Complete your profile

Account Management

Learn how to manage your profile settings:

  • Update your personal information
  • Change your password
  • Manage notification preferences
  • Set up two-factor authentication

Events

Follow these steps to create a new event:

  1. Go to your dashboard
  2. Click "Create Event"
  3. Fill in event details
  4. Set funding goals
  5. Add images and documents
  6. Publish your event

Sponsorships

Learn how to sponsor events:

  1. Browse available events
  2. Review event details
  3. Choose sponsorship amount
  4. Complete payment
  5. Track your sponsorship

Payments

  • Bank Transfer
  • PayPal
  • Cryptocurrency

Security

Protect your account with these security features:

  • Two-factor authentication
  • Strong password requirements
  • Login activity monitoring
  • Secure payment processing
