General Questions

EduSponsor+ is a platform that connects educational institutions with corporate sponsors to facilitate the organization and funding of educational events. We help institutions find sponsors for their events and enable companies to support educational initiatives.

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, choose your role (institution or sponsor), and complete your profile. Institutions can then create events and seek sponsors, while sponsors can browse events and provide funding.

For Educational Institutions

You can create various types of educational events including:
  • Conferences and seminars
  • Workshops and training sessions
  • Hackathons and coding competitions
  • Career fairs and networking events
  • Research presentations

To create an event:
  1. Log in to your account
  2. Click on "Create Event"
  3. Fill in the event details (title, description, date, location, etc.)
  4. Set your funding goal
  5. Submit the event for review

For Sponsors

As a sponsor, you can:
  • Browse available events
  • Filter events by category, location, or funding amount
  • Review event details and proposals
  • Communicate with event organizers
  • Provide funding for selected events

Sponsoring educational events offers several benefits:
  • Build brand awareness among students and educators
  • Access to talented students for recruitment
  • Support educational initiatives in your community
  • Network with other sponsors and institutions
  • Tax benefits for educational sponsorships

Payment and Security

We use industry-standard security measures to protect all transactions:
  • SSL encryption for all data transmission
  • Secure payment processing through trusted providers
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Compliance with data protection regulations

We accept various payment methods including:
  • Credit and debit cards
  • Bank transfers
  • Digital wallets
  • Corporate payment solutions
